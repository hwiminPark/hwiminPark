### 💼 Selected Projects
1. **Residential Water Demand Modeling**: Designed and implemented econometric models to estimate residential water demand in Calgary using historical data from 2011–2024. Analyzed the effects of population, weather, and price on consumption using level-level and log-log specifications. Found water demand to be price-inelastic but strongly influenced by temperature and population growth. Developed an interactive Streamlit tool to simulate future demand scenarios and support municipal planning and pricing policy.
2. **Bid-Ask Spread Prediction**: Developed machine learning models to predict bid-ask spreads in the options market using intraday data from AAPL, GME, and SPY (650k+ observations). Engineered liquidity-based features such as open interest, moneyness, and time-to-expiry, and evaluated model performance using RMSE and R². Achieved high accuracy (R² = 0.94) with Random Forests, significantly outperforming linear baselines. Built a Streamlit dashboard for real-time spread forecasting and interactive liquidity analysis to support retail trading strategies.
3. **Credit Card Offer Acceptance**: Designed a predictive model to estimate the likelihood of customers accepting credit card offers based on their demographic and financial profiles. The project focused on identifying key factors influencing acceptance to enhance marketing strategies and improve the effectiveness of promotional campaigns.
